There are a lot of interpretations for soul but most agree that it is the ethereal substance, the non-material part of body. Some name it the breath of universe.

Body is named as tabernacle of soul. So, I guess people who lost their minds and become stone symbolise their inability to protect their souls. The real souls of body, for not protecting themselves, and the soul for taking someone else's body.

Blood is often associated withlife,death,redemption, innocence,massacre,malediction.I think in a way, it is connected to reflection of souls.

Water popularly represents life and has been used to communicate the sacred value of life.So, powers related to it, I guess, directly affects the lives of both the user and affected side.This might be why it requires lots of energy.

The water dragons are healers, warriors, and peace gathering clans. The water dragon is said to bring peace and unity where only war was before.
